How We Got Started

Greenville's explosive growth over the past decade created a gap in the market: homeowners and businesses needed dependable junk removal that didn't involve vague pricing or missed appointments. We started small, serving neighborhoods around Downtown Greenville and West Greenville, learning quickly that customers valued transparency and respect for their property above everything else.

As word spread, we expanded throughout the Upstate—handling everything from construction debris in Hollingsworth Park's new builds to furniture removal for families downsizing from Cleveland Park area homes. The work taught us that every job is different: a garage cleanout requires different planning than a post-renovation haul, and customers appreciate when you understand those distinctions.

Today we serve residential and commercial clients across the region, maintaining the same standards we started with: clear communication, fair pricing, and a commitment to donating or recycling whenever possible. The Upstate has been good to us, and we take pride in keeping it clean.
