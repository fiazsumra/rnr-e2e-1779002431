Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day appliance removal in Greenville?
Yes, we frequently offer same-day service when you call early in the day. During slower periods we can sometimes accommodate even afternoon calls for same-day pickup. Next-day service is almost always available.
Will you disconnect my appliance before removing it?
Absolutely. We handle basic disconnections for washers, dryers, dishwashers, and similar appliances as part of our service. For gas appliances or complex built-ins, we'll let you know if a licensed plumber or electrician should handle disconnection first.
What do you do with old appliances after removal?
Working appliances are donated to local charities whenever possible. Non-working units go to recycling facilities where metals are reclaimed and refrigerants are properly recovered. We follow all EPA and state environmental regulations for appliance disposal.
Can you remove built-in appliances in Greenville homes?
Yes, we regularly remove built-in dishwashers, microwaves, and other integrated appliances. The process takes a bit longer than freestanding units, but our team has the tools and experience to extract them carefully without damaging cabinetry or countertops.
Do I need to be present during appliance removal?
It's helpful if you're available to show us the appliance location and answer any questions, but if you need to leave after we arrive, that's usually fine. Many customers leave us a key or garage code for scheduled pickups while they're at work.
How much notice do you need for appliance removal service?
We can often schedule same-day or next-day appointments. For larger jobs involving multiple appliances or commercial equipment, a day or two notice helps us allocate the right crew size, but we're flexible and work with your timeline.
Is there anything I need to do before you arrive?
Just make sure there's a clear path to the appliance and that it's empty of any contents. If you can disconnect water or electrical yourself you're welcome to, but that's not required—we handle it as part of the service.
