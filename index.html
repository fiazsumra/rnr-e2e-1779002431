Frequently Asked Questions

How do you price junk removal?
Pricing is based on the volume your items take up in our truck and the labor required. We provide a free, no-obligation quote when we arrive and see exactly what needs to go. You approve the price before we start loading. There are no hidden fees for stairs, disposal, or mileage within our service area.
Do I need to be present during the removal?
Not necessarily. As long as the items are clearly marked or we have access to the removal area, many customers leave a key or garage code and go about their day. We'll text or call when we're finished and send photos if you'd like confirmation.
What items can't you take?
We can't haul hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, asbestos, or fuel. Most household and business junk is fair game—furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and general clutter. If you're unsure about a specific item, just ask when you call.
How quickly can you come out?
We often have same-day or next-day availability, depending on your location and our schedule. During busy seasons—especially after severe weather or at the end of the month when people are moving—it's smart to call a day or two ahead. But if you need us urgently, we'll do our best to make it happen.
Do you recycle or donate items?
Absolutely. We work with local donation centers and recycling facilities to keep usable furniture, working appliances, and recyclable materials out of the landfill. If something is in decent shape, we'll give it a second life. Items that can't be reused are disposed of responsibly according to local regulations.
What if I need regular junk removal for my business?
We handle recurring commercial accounts for property management companies, retail stores, offices, and warehouses. We can set up weekly, monthly, or on-call service that fits your needs. Just let us know your schedule and volume, and we'll build a plan that works.
